The half-vectorization of an
$
k \times k
$
matrix
$
\mathbf{A}
$,
given by
$
\mathrm{vech}
\left(
\mathbf{A}
\right)
$,
is the
$
\frac{1}{2}
k
\left( 
k 
+ 
1 
\right)
\times
1
$
vector obtained from
the vectorization of 
$
\mathbf{A}
$,
given by
$
\mathrm{vec}
\left(
\mathbf{A}
\right)
$,
where that all upper diagonal elements of
$
\mathbf{A}
$
are eliminated.
